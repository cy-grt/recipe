Recipe

Chocolat cake

"This easy chocolate cake recipe is a great classic. It will please the most greedy of you."

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10min 25min 35min

Ingredients

Dark chocolate : 200g

Sugar : 150g

Butter : 200g

Eggs : 4

Flour : 80g

Preparation

1

 Melt chocolate with butter

2

 Mix sugar, eggs and flour

3

 Mix everything together to make a paste

4

 Put in the oven
